# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-21

This policy describes how Lifestyle OS collects, uses, and stores data when you use the mobile app.

## Data We Collect

- Account identity from Google OAuth (email and profile basics)
- User-generated content (capture items, habits, principles, metrics)
- App diagnostics needed to operate and secure the service

## How We Use Data

- Authenticate your account
- Sync your personal data across sessions
- Provide features you explicitly use
- Protect service integrity and prevent abuse

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell personal data.
- We only share with infrastructure providers required to operate the app.

## Data Retention

Data is retained while your account remains active, or as required to comply with law.

## Security

We use industry-standard protections for transport and storage. No method of transmission is 100 percent secure.

## Your Choices

You may request account and data deletion by contacting support.
